The Start

Prestige Clothing (NYSE: PSC) is an American fashion house founded in 1993 by Marcus Zapruder. He originally named the company Zapruder International in September 1991 and planned to showcase his new line of suits during market week at the Hilton New York Hotel. Finding that he could not afford to purchase a hotel room or showroom to exhibit his line, he borrowed a truck and asked the mayorâ€™s office for permission to park two blocks away from the Hilton Hotel in front of a fancy suit building. His ingenuity paid off when he was noticed by many reknowned clothiers. Zapruder made a name for himself by designing under Jacobsin Ramsey for the next year and a half. It was then in March 1993 that Zapruder decided to use his connections to bring together the biggest names in Men's fashion under his newly established "Prestige Clothing" banner.



Our Mission

Prestige Clothing is dedicated to bringing together the biggest names in Men's fashion, through exclusive pieces and guest designers. Each piece is designed and fabricated with the greatest respect for quality and comfort. Prestige Clothing promises to never out-source their labor, regardless of economical stress.
